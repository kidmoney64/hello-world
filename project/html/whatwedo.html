What We Do

Here at Like Mother Like Daughter: A Doula Duo Keli and Jocelyn are here for you in each stage of your journey into parenthood. Their goal is to provide exceptional care for mothers and fathers-to-be as they transition into ‘mom and dad’. They want to make sure birthers and their partners know all their options and have the support they need for however they choose to give birth and take care of their new bundles of joy. Keli and Jocelyn want to empower women by giving them any and all information they may need to achieve the dream of how they envision their pregnancy and beyond to go. We are here to show you techniques and how/when to use them in order to make it through this difficult, but beautiful, time in your life.

“In giving birth to our babies, we may find that we give birth to new possibilities within ourselves” -Myla & Jon Kabat-Zinn
