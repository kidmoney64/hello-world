Our Services

Here at Like Mother Like Daughter: A Doula Duo is here to serve your every need through pregnancy and beyond! Listed below are the services we provide and prices may vary.

  • Lamaze/Childbirth classes
  • Birth Doula :Hiring us to be your Birth Doula gives you the support and advocacy you need to get through any kind of birth you may choose. Whether it’s a water birth, home birth, or in a hospital, we will be there for you every step of the way. We want to be a valuable addition to your birthing team that gives you the encouragement you need.
  • Postpartum Consultations:We are dedicated and focused on the “4thtrimester” which is described as when you bond the most with your new baby and learn how to become a new family. The Postpartum Doula service is here to provide you with emotional and physical support to transition into this wonderful new time in your life.
  • Lactation Consultations
  • Newborn Care
